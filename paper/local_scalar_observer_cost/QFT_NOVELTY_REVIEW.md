## Result Under Review

result starts after that channel is fixed. For half-line momentum data with
final support `[0,L]`, inverse temperature `beta`, dephasing exponent `Gamma`,
and post-switch field energy `E`, the paper proves

```text
Gamma <= E C_beta(L),
C_beta(L)=2 L Lambda(pi L/beta).
```

patch, the Bunch-Davies relation `beta=2 pi R` makes the same s-wave momentum
profile the unique optimum over every angular and canonical sector.
