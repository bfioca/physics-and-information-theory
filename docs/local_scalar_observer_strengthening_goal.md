   momentum coefficient, unique optimizer, global bracket, and uniform

2. **de Sitter specialization.** Prove that at `beta=2 pi R` angular resolvent
   order and coordinate-sector estimates make the half-line s-wave momentum
   profile the unique full-phase-space optimizer.
3. **Sharpness language.** State the fixed-final-support theorem first and the
   source-cylinder result only as a causal-envelope corollary.
4. **Physical naming.** Use post-switch scalar-field energy throughout and
   keep actuator work, probe stress, and coupled gravity outside the theorem.
5. **Proof audit.** Check independently the angular ordering, coordinate
   domination, strict uniqueness, both remainder estimates, and smooth-source
   closure.
6. **Two-domain novelty review.** Obtain one written assessment from a
   relativistic detector/QFT specialist and one from an integral-operator,
   Riesz-potential, or fractional-Sobolev specialist.
7. **Submission artifact.** Integrate review corrections and freeze a clean
   tagged PDF, source archive, certificate, and replay.

## Decision Rule

Issue **SUBMIT** only if Gates 1-7 close and neither specialist identifies the
reflected KMS operator, full-sector reduction, and support asymptotics as an
immediate established corollary. Issue **STRENGTHEN** only for a concrete,
bounded missing theorem identified by review. Issue **NO-GO** if the central
conjunction is already standard or too routine for a standalone article.

Fixed-source-cylinder controllability, a relativistic probe-field
realization, and dynamical gravity are follow-up directions unless a reviewer
identifies one as necessary for the narrow paper.
