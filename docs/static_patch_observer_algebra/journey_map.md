## What Not To Lead With

The earlier stabilizer/QEC and bridge-channel artifacts are useful provenance,
but they are not the clean external story anymore. They should support the
static-patch observer-algebra package rather than compete with it.

Do not lead with:

- literal de Sitter ER=EPR;
- a claimed dS/CFT dual;
- novelty claims about standard stabilizer or UHF facts;
- a one-off finite toy model.

Lead with the focused operator-algebra question:

```text
What finite operational data determine the observer's algebra rather than only
its screen shadow?
```
